Buena Vista Social Club

Synopsis
Based on the Grammy Award-winning 1997 Cuban album and documentary, this musical follows legendary singers Omara Portuondo and Ibrahim Ferrer and their fellow musicians as they are reunited in Havana by American producer Ry Cooder to record an album that would make them world-famous. Saheem Ali directs with choreography by Justin Peck and Patricia Delgado.
