Proof

Synopsis
David Auburn's Pulitzer- and Tony-winning play follows Catherine, the brilliant daughter of a renowned but mentally ill mathematician who has just died, as she faces questions about whether she authored a major mathematical proof and wrestles with her own fear of inheriting her father's illness. Directed by Thomas Kail, this revival stars Ayo Edebiri and Don Cheadle in their Broadway debuts.
